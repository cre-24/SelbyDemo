About Selby Incorporated

Selby Incorporated is a third-generation family-owned machining job shop dedicated to precision manufacturing and custom machining solutions. With decades of expertise passed down through the family, the company brings deep knowledge and craftsmanship to every project it undertakes.

As a specialized job shop, Selby Incorporated serves a diverse client base across industries, providing custom machining services tailored to meet unique specifications and demanding applications. The company's commitment to quality, precision, and reliability has been built over generations, establishing it as a trusted partner for complex machining work.

The team at Selby Incorporated combines traditional machining expertise with attention to detail, ensuring that each component meets exacting standards. Whether handling prototype work, production runs, or specialized custom fabrication, the company approaches every job with the same dedication to excellence that has defined its reputation.

Selby Incorporated represents the backbone of American manufacturing—a family business with roots in the trade, committed to delivering quality workmanship and personalized service that larger facilities simply cannot match.
